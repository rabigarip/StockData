Company Profile

ADES Holding Company Listed JSC is Saudi Arabia-based offshore drilling service provider. The Company owns and operates portfolio of offshore and onshore rigs across the Middle East and Asia. Its main activities include contracting services for excavation and maintenance of onshore and offshore wells, services related to oil extraction, excavation and onshore and offshore maintenance services, provision of contracted offshore and onshore excavation services, and provision of maintenance and production services.
