Company Profile

Thimar Development Holding Company SJSC is a Saudi Arabia-based company, which is engaged in the management of subsidiaries, investment of funds attributable to subsidiaries, holding the underlying real estate and movable properties, providing subsidiaries with borrowings, guarantees and funds. The Company operates through a subsidiary, Wasmi and Thimar Meat Company which is engaged in fresh meat production, wholesale and retail of meat and meat products.
