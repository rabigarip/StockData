Company Profile

Company for Cooperative Insurance SJSC is a Saudi Arabia-based insurance company, which is engaged in the cooperative insurance operations and all related activities including reinsurance and agency activities. The Company operates in following segments: Medical, which provides coverage for health insurance; Medical Umrah, which provides coverage for health insurance for pilgrims; Motor, which provides coverage for motor insurance and for Manafeth third party liability insurance for foreign vehicles; Property and casualty that includes coverage for property, engineering, marine, aviation, energy and general accidents insurance; General accidents that includes Hajj & Umrah insurance, coverage for pilgrims; Travel and Coronavirus disease( COVID)-19 ,which includes coverage of compulsory travel insurance in addition to some coverages related to COVID-19 for citizens travelling abroad; and Protection and savings.
