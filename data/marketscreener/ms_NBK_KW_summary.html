Company Profile

National Bank of Kuwait SAKP is a Kuwait based bank. The Bank is organized into six segments which include consumer Banking, corporate banking, NBK wealth, Islamic banking, group Centre, international banking. Consumer banking segment provides loans, credit cards, deposits, foreign exchange and other branch related services, corporate banking segment provides lending, deposits, trade finance, foreign exchange and advisory services, NBK wealth segment provides asset management, custody, brokerage, lending, deposits and other banking services to individuals and institutional clients, Islamic banking segment represents the financial results of Boubyan Bank K.S.C.P, Group Centre segment provides treasury, investments, and other Group activities, International banking segment provides lending, deposits, trade finance to corporate and individual customers at Groupâ€™s locations.
