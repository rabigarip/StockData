Company Profile

Saudi Aramco Base Oil Company - Luberef SJSC is a Saudi Arabia-based base oil producer. The company is engaged in constructing, owning, and operating refineries for lubricating oils, as well as purchasing, selling, transporting, marketing, importing, and exporting lubricating oils, additives, lubricating oil blending stocks, by-products, and other related petroleum products. The company operates in the Jeddah and Yanbu regions of Saudi Arabia and has operations in Hamriyah, United Arab Emirates. It is organized as a single business unit aligned with its principal business activity. The Its product categories of base oils include aramcoDURA, aramcoPRIMA, and aramcoULTRA. Saudi Aramco is the immediate parent of the Company.
