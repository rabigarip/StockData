Company Profile

Saudi Investment Bank SJSC is a Saudi Arabia-based company that offers commercial and retail banking services. The Company operates in following segments Retail banking, which includes loans, deposits, and other products for public institutions, high-net worth individuals and consumers; Corporate banking, which includes loans, deposits and other credit products for corporate, small to medium-sized businesses; Treasury and Investments that includes money market, investments and treasury services, and investments in associates and related activities; Asset management and brokerage that includes dealing, managing, advising and custody of securities services; and Other that includes support functions, special credit, and other management and control units. The Company also offers Shariah compliant banking products and services.
