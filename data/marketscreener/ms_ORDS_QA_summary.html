Company Profile

Commercial Bank PSQC is a Qatar-based financial institution that provided banking solutions. The company operates in three segments: Wholesale Banking, which offers a wide range of conventional funded and non-funded credit facilities, deposit services, currency exchange, derivatives trading, loan syndication, and manages money market funds and investments; Retail Banking, which provides personal banking services including accounts, cards, loans, mortgages, custodial and brokerage services through its subsidiary Commercial bank Financial Services L.L.C. in Qatar; and International, which includes banking services through its subsidiary Alternatif Bank in Turkey and strategic investments in the National Bank of Oman and United Arab Bank in the UAE. Its subsidiaries include Alternatif bank A.S, Commercial Bank Financial Services L.L.C., CBQ Finance Limited, CB Global Trading Limited, among others.
