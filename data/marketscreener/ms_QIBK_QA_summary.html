Company Profile

Qatar Islamic Bank QPSC is a Qatar based Islamic bank which is primarily engaged in corporate, retail and investment banking in accordance with Islamic sharia rules. The Company is organized into four reportable segments which include corporate banking, personal banking, group function and Local and International subsidiaries. Coporate Banking segment is engaged in services offered to institutional investors, corporates, small and medium enterprises, financial institutions and investment vehicles. Personal banking segment is engaged in services that are offered to individual customers through local branches of the bank. Group function segment is engaged in providing Treasury, investment, finance and other central functions.
