Company Profile

Rasan Information Technology Company SCJSC is a Saudi Arabia-based FinTech and InsurTech services provider. The Company provides insurance and financial services through their subsidiaries: Tameeni allows customers to compare, buy, or renew insurance from network of insurance providers; Treza provides insurance solutions specifically designed for motor leasing providers and their customers; Awalmazad is the online auto auction platform that is handling salvage and repossessed vehicles for insurance companies and leasing providers, and Warshti provides a range of car maintenance and care services.
