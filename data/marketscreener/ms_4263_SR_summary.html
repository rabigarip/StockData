Company Profile

SAL Saudi Logistics Services Company SJSC is a Saudi Arabia-based logistics service provider. The Company provides cargo ground handling services at airport terminals, freight brokerage services, warehouse management services, administrative services and storage services. The Company operates in following segments: Air cargo handling, which includes cargo handling services to air cargo carriers operating at the Kingdom's airport; Logistic solutions, which include end to end logistic solutions services, customs clearance, warehousing and inventory management; and Fulfilment , which includes warehouse management solutions. The Company operates through approximately 14 branches.
