Company Profile

Savola Group Company SJSC is a Saudi Arabia-based industrial company for the food and retail sectors. It is involved in the manufacturing and sale of vegetable oils and retail outlets, dairy products, fast foods, exports and imports, commercial contracting, trade agencies, development of agricultural products and real estate related investment activities. It operates five segments ; Food Processing, which includes manufacturing, sale and distribution of edible oils, sugar, pasta, spices, nuts, pulses and other food products; The Retail, which includes hyper markets, supermarkets and convenience stores operations; The Food Services that includes food products and fast food restaurantsâ€™ chain operated by Herfy; The Frozen Food that includes manufacturing, wholesale and retail distribution of frozen food products operated by Good Food Company; and The Frozen Food that includes manufacturing, wholesale and retail distribution of frozen food products operated by Good Food Company.
