Company Profile

Bank Muscat SAOG is an Oman-based bank. The Bank is engaged in commercial and investment banking activities through a network of approximately 190 branches. The Bank is organized into five business segments: Corporate banking offers services to business and corporate customers, including lending, deposit-taking, trade finance, foreign exchange, transaction banking, cash management, and other related services; Personal banking provides consumer loans, credit cards, deposit accounts including savings deposits, foreign exchange, e-banking, remittances, bancassurance, and other branch-related services to individuals; Wholesale Banking includes treasury, financial institutions, investments, advisory, and asset management services; International banking includes activities of overseas branches, representative offices, and investments outside Oman; and Islamic banking represents the banking activities of the Bank's Islamic window Meethaq. The bank also provides insurance services.
