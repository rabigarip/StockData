Company Profile

Qatar International Islamic Bank QPSC is a Qatar-based Islamic bank. The Bank is engaged in banking, financing, and investing activities in accordance with Islamic Shariah rules and principles. The Bank operates in three segments: Corporate Banking, which includes financing, deposits, and other transactions and balances with corporate customers, government, and semi-government institutions; Personal Banking, which includes financing, deposits, and other transactions and balances with retail customers; and Treasury & Investments, which undertakes the Bankâ€™s funding and centralized risk management activities through borrowings, issuance of Sukuk, use of risk management instruments for risk mitigation purposes, and investment in liquid assets such as short-term deposits and corporate and government Sukuk.
