Company Profile

First Abu Dhabi Bank PJSC is a United Arab Emirates-based bank. It operates through following segments: Investment Banking, which offers banking and financing solutions, including corporate and Islamic finance, capital markets, transaction banking, trade, liquidity, and cash management services, among others; Corporate and Commercial Banking, which provides corporate banking, contracting, commercial banking and privileged client group; Global Markets trading , which includes trading, market-making, risk management and investment management activities, among others; Consumer Banking , which include conventional and Islamic sector that include current accounts, deposits, credit cards, loans, and wealth products, among others; Global Private Banking, which include banking products, investment solutions, brokerage and securities services; and Head office, which provides centralized human resources, information technology, operations, and finance, among others.
