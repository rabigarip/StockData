Company Profile

Abu Dhabi Islamic Bank PJSC (ADIB) is a United Arab Emirates-based entity which is engaged in providing banking services, financing and investing activities in accordance with Islamic Sharia. The Company operates in following segments; Global retail banking which includes small and medium businesses and individual customers deposits; Global wholesale banking which includes financing , other credit facilities, deposits and current accounts for corporate and institutional customers; private banking which includes financing , other credit facilities, deposits and current accounts for individual customers; Treasury which includes money market, trading and treasury; Real estate which includes subsidiaries of the bank handling the acquisition, selling, development and leasing; Associates and Subsidiaries which include Banks subsidiaries, associates and joint ventures; Other operations which includes operations comprises of Head Office including unallocated costs.
