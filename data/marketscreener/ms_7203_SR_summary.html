Company Profile

Al Nahdi Medical Company SJSC is a Saudi Arabia-based company, which is engaged in the wholesale and retail trading of cosmetics, pharmaceutical products, special and healthy foods, and medical equipment. The Company operates two segments: The Front Shop and Pharma. The Company also operates specialized medical clinics through one of its subsidiaries.
