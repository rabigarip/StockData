Company Profile

Elm Company SJSC, formerly Al Elm Information Security Company CJSC, provides information security services, consulting services, exchanging credit information, and managing and operating data and information centres, among others. The Company operates in following segments: Digital business, which offers ready-to-use solutions in the form of technical services, portals, electronic applications, and related support work, among others; Business process outsourcing, which offers business attribution solutions in the field of operation and service; and Professional services, which include advisory services, and professional services in data analysis and artificial intelligence, among others.
