Company Profile

Modern Mills Company SJSC is a Saudi Arabia-based entity which is primarily engaged in flour and animal feed manufacturing. The Company is organized into three geographical segments which include Khamis, Al Jumum, and Al Jowf. The Company is selling various products of flour, feed and bran. The Company has one subsidiary namely Hasad Al-Arabia, also it has three branches within Saudi Arabia.
