Company Profile

Mabanee Company KPSC is a Kuwait-based real estate and construction company. The Company is primarily engaged in construction, including prefabricated buildings, real estate development, and infrastructure projects. The Company invests in property management, commercial complexes, and financial portfolios and also the Company operates hotels, shopping malls, exhibitions, and related services, contributing to urban development. The Company is organized into two segments which include Construction and real estate segment: consist of leasing of properties, real estate development, projects leasing and the construction activity for self or others. hotel segment: consist of the hotel services provided through Hilton Garden Inn Hotel and Waldorf Astoria Hotel, Kuwait.
