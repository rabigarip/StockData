Company Profile

Emaar Properties PJSC is a United Arab Emirates based company which is engaged in property investment, development and development management, shopping malls and retail, hospitality, property management and utility services and investments in providers of financial services. The Company is organized into three segments which include real estate, leasing, retail and related activities, hospitality. real estate segment is engaged in developing, selling and managing condominiums, villas, commercial units and plots of land. leasing, retail and related activities segment is engaged in developing, leasing and managing malls, retail, commercial and residential spaces. Hospitality segment is engaged in developing, owning and managing hotels, serviced apartments and leisure activities.
